What is mechanical engineering?

Mechanical engineering is the study that uses engineering physics, engineering mathematics, and materials science principles to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems. Mechanical engineers are able to use heat and mechanical power for the design, production, and operation of machines and tools. Mechanical engineers can work on anything like a rocketship or something like a car.
